Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

At MatchTagR, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally

MatchTagR stores all data locally on your device. This includes:

  • Match events and timestamps
  • Video references and metadata
  • Sport preferences and settings
  • Team names and match configurations

1.2 No Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers. All data remains on your device and is never sent to external servers unless you explicitly choose to share it.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • We do not have access to your data
  • We do not analyze or process your data
  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • Your data is only used within the app for its intended functionality

3. Data Sharing

You have full control over sharing your data:

  • You can share match events via email, messages, or social media using iOS sharing features
  • You can export videos and data using standard iOS export functionality
  • Any sharing is done through your device's native sharing mechanisms, not through our servers

4. Photo Library Access

MatchTagR may request access to your Photo Library to:

  • Import videos for tagging
  • Save exported highlight reels (if you choose to save them)

This access is optional and only used when you explicitly choose to import or save content. We do not access or transmit any photos or videos from your library without your explicit action.

5. Camera Access

If you use the "Record & Tag" mode, MatchTagR will request camera access to record matches. This access is only used for recording within the app and is not shared with any external services.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures
  • We recommend using device encryption and passcodes to protect your data
  • When you delete the app, all associated data is removed from your device

7. Children's Privacy

MatchTagR is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored in the app (it's all visible within the app)
  • Delete any data at any time (through the app's delete functions)
  • Uninstall the app, which removes all associated data from your device

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: privacy@matchtagr.com
Support: support@matchtagr.com

By using MatchTagR, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
