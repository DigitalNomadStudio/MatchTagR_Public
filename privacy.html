Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

Simple Summary: MatchTagR does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal information. All your match data and videos stay on your device.

Information Collection and Use

MatchTagR is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect any personal information from our users.

Data Storage

All match data, including:

  • Event tags and timestamps
  • Match videos (recorded or imported)
  • Sport selections and custom tags
  • Match settings and preferences
  • Purchase information (stored by Apple)

...is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage. Videos are stored in your device's file system. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Data Sharing

When you use sharing features:

  • Event Reports: Images are generated on your device and shared via iOS native sharing
  • Highlight Reels: Videos are created on your device and saved to your photo library or shared via iOS
  • You control where and how to share using iOS native sharing
  • We do not receive, store, or have access to any shared data
  • All sharing is handled entirely by iOS

Analytics and Tracking

MatchTagR does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Tracking technologies
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party services (except Apple's StoreKit for purchases)

In-App Purchases

MatchTagR uses Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases:

  • Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple
  • We do not collect or store payment information
  • Apple manages all payment processing and receipts
  • Purchase status is verified through Apple's servers

Network Activity

MatchTagR only uses network connectivity for:

  • Loading in-app purchase products from Apple's servers
  • Verifying purchase receipts with Apple

All core features work completely offline. No match data, videos, or personal information is transmitted over the network.

Permissions

MatchTagR requests the following permissions for specific features:

  • Camera access: Required for Record & Tag Match mode to record video
  • Microphone access: Required for video recording and speech-to-text tagging
  • Photo library access: Required to import videos, save highlight reels, and share event reports
  • Speech recognition: Required for speech-to-text custom event tagging

All permissions are optional and only requested when you use features that require them. The app will explain why each permission is needed before requesting it.

MatchTagR does NOT request:

  • Location services
  • Contacts
  • Calendar access
  • Health data

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Individual events: Delete events from the events list
  • Match data: Clear all events for a specific mode
  • Videos: Delete videos from your photo library or app storage
  • All app data: Delete the app from your device

When you delete the app, all associated data (events, videos, settings) is permanently removed from your device. Purchase history is maintained by Apple and can be restored if you reinstall the app.

Children's Privacy

MatchTagR does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
