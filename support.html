About MatchTagR

MatchTagR offers three powerful modes to capture and analyze match events. Whether you're tagging live, reviewing footage, or recording the match yourself, MatchTagR has you covered.

Three Modes

  • Live Tag Match: Tag events in real-time during a live match and sync with video later
  • Tag Uploaded Video: Import existing footage and tag events while watching
  • Record & Tag Match: Record the match with your camera while tagging events simultaneously

Key Features

  • Smart Tagging: One-tap tagging with speech-to-text for custom events
  • Highlight Reels: Automatically generate video highlights from tagged events
  • Multiple Sports: Pre-configured tags for soccer, basketball, and more
  • Custom Tags: Create your own event types for any sport
  • Beautiful Reports: Share professional event summaries
  • Privacy First: All data and videos stored locally on your device
  • Works Offline: Full functionality without internet connection

Getting Started

First Time Setup

  1. Choose Your Sport: Select your sport to get pre-configured event tags
  2. Grant Permissions: Allow camera, microphone, and speech recognition for full functionality
  3. Start Free Trial: Try all Pro features free for 14 days

Live Tag Match Mode

  1. Select Sport: Choose your sport or use existing match data
  2. Start Match: Tap START when the match begins
  3. Tag Events: Tap event buttons or use speech-to-text for custom events
  4. Sync Video: After the match, import video to sync with your tags
  5. Create Highlights: Generate highlight reels from tagged events

Tag Uploaded Video Mode

  1. Import Video: Select a video from your photo library
  2. Tag While Watching: Play the video and tag events as they happen
  3. Create Highlights: Generate highlight reels from your tags

Record & Tag Match Mode

  1. Start Recording: Begin recording with your device camera
  2. Tag While Recording: Tag events in real-time as you record
  3. Review & Share: Watch your recording and create highlight reels

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial?

The 14-day free trial includes full access to all Pro features: all three modes (Live Tag, Video Tag, Record & Tag), highlight reel creation, speech-to-text tagging, and all sports.

What's the difference between Basic and Pro?

Basic Edition ($5.99): Live Tag Match mode only, all sports, unlimited events, and event sharing.

Pro Edition ($14.99): All three modes, highlight reel creation, speech-to-text tagging, and all features.

How do I create highlight reels?

After tagging events in Video Tag or Record & Tag mode, tap "Create Highlight Reel" to automatically generate a video compilation of your tagged moments. You can select which events to include.

Can I use speech-to-text for custom events?

Yes! In Pro Edition, tap the microphone button and speak your event description. The app will transcribe it and create a custom event tag.

Can I edit or delete events?

Yes! Tap "View Events" to see all recorded events. You can:

  • Edit events: Tap on an event to edit its description and time
  • Delete individual events: Swipe left on any event and tap Delete
  • Clear all events: Use the menu to clear all events for a fresh start

What sports are supported?

MatchTagR comes with pre-configured tags for soccer/football, basketball, and other sports. You can also create custom tags for any sport.

How do I sync video with live tags?

After tagging events in Live Tag Match mode, tap "Sync Video" and import your match footage. The app will align your tags with the video timeline.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All data and videos are stored locally on your device. MatchTagR doesn't collect, transmit, or share any information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does MatchTagR work offline?

Yes! MatchTagR works completely offline. No internet connection required for any features.

Can I use this for multiple sports?

Yes! You can switch between sports and create custom event tags for any sport you coach or follow.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Use Live Tag mode at the field when you can't record video - sync footage later
  • Record & Tag for instant analysis - perfect for training sessions

  • Create custom tags for your team's specific plays and tactics
  • Use speech-to-text for quick custom events without typing
  • Generate highlight reels to share with players after matches
  • Keep your device charged - video recording uses battery quickly
